Dr. Sam Quill is a private tutor in English, History, Philosophy, and Critical Thinking, specialising in scholarship exams and Oxbridge entrance.

Sam Quill holds a First from Oxford in English, an M.Phil. from Cambridge, and a Ph.D. from Queen Mary, University of London, where he also taught and earned a Certificate in Learning and Teaching with Distinction. He is an Associate Fellow of the Higher Education Academy. A full-time private tutor since 2019, he specialises in scholarship examinations at major British public schools, Oxbridge entrance, A-level and GCSE tuition, and personal statements. He also offers the Salon de Conversation, one-to-one tutorials for adults in literature, philosophy, theology, and history. Beyond teaching, Sam is a published poet, a musician, and a reviewer for the Times Literary Supplement.
