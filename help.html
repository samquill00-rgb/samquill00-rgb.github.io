May I help you?

Teaching

  • Tutoring

    Private tuition in English, History, Philosophy and Critical Thinking. Specialist in public school scholarship exams (13+), Oxbridge entrance, A-level and GCSE. Six scholarship places won to Westminster, four to Winchester.

  • Salon de Conversation

    Adult education in literature, philosophy, theology and history, one hour at a time. Sessions available à la carte or as a short course. A free taster session is on offer. Suitable for individuals, gifts, or a private group.

Records, Writing, Reviewing

  • Music

    Solo work available on Spotify. Also the lyricist and vocalist for Points of Light, a collaborative project described by Boomkat as “a serene and otherworldly suite.”

  • Poetry

    Published poet. Works include Hey Ho The White Swan By God I Am Thy Man (Broken Sleep Books, 2022) and poems in PN Review, the London Review of Books Bookshop Blog, and Poetry Birmingham Literary Journal, among others. Forthcoming in an anthology from Broken Sleep Books (2026).

  • Grub Street

    Criticism, journalism and prose. Reviews published in the Times Literary Supplement; a chapter on the Peterloo Massacre in English Radical History (Pen and Sword, 2021); and a defence of poetry for the Newcastle Centre for the Literary Arts.
